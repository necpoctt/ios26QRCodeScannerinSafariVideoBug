iOS 26 Video Bug – Opened via Built-in QR Code Scanner in Safari<

Steps to Reproduce:

  1. On iOS 26 device, use the built-in Camera app to scan a QR code that points to: https://relaxed-brioche-3fd908.netlify.app/
    2. (A minimal HTML page with a video element and MP4 source.)
  2. Tap the banner that appears after scanning to open the page.
  3. The video loads and plays normally on the first attempt.
  4. Close the page, then scan the same QR code again and open it.
